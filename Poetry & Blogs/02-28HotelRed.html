28 Feb / By: Edgar Cuevas

HotelRed

Stood outside ready to cry
Ran up to my room to scream
At that man staring in the mirror
Scared I won't make it past 25
Wish I could die in my dream
As I blackout off stale liquor

Late night drives on Lake Shore
All those emotions I can't ignore
Said you'd never be with me
Until I came to terms with my heartbeat
Never could pass all your tests
So HotelRed is where my soul rests

One more drink
My heart will sink
Dreaming of you
Back in June
When it got serious
When we both became delirious
That cold December
Is all I could remember
Engaged at eighteen
But single at nineteen

HotelRed
