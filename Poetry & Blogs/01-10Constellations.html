10 Jan / By: Edgar Cuevas

Constellations

When the hope is gone
And friendships begin to rust
Call me to loosen ends up
Take my heart from my sleeve
If you’d think, I’d ever leave
You’ve been on the run
Just to feel high off the rush
But there’s a bright sun
Illuminating you so much
And I’m sorry that you feel like this
It’s not your fault with what happened before
You shouldn’t be left alone like this
Craving your love, afraid you’ll be gone out the door
Felt so close to you so naturally
Force of nature, love without anatomy
Shaping out all we could ever be
Let my heart do the paving
Light, you up to form a constellation
