Brotherhood

Defining what a brotherhood is can only tell so much. To truly understand what brotherhood is, you have to experience it. At Delta Sigma Phi one thing brings us together, and that is each other. Without one another we are weak, but together we are strong. Being a Delta Sig means that wherever you go, you are wanted and valued. Our chapter may be known by location, but our brotherhood has no boundaries.
