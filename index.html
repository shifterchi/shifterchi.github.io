Quick History

Delta Sigma Phi was founded in 1899 at the City College of New York. Since then, the ideals of brotherhood, scholarship, and philanthropy have been spreading throughout the nation in the form of new chapters and expanded membership. The Alpha Alpha chapter was established at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign in 1919. Today, we continue to be a chapter that prides itself on maximizing it's member's potential in the social and academic aspects of collegiate life. For over 90 years, we have taken college students and turned them into leaders amongst their peers. We are always looking for students with potential to join our brotherhood here at Delta Sigma Phi.
